Detailed Description

Represents a single scene in the game, handling its entities, lifecycle, and rendering.

Scene encapsulates all game entities, managing their lifecycle from initialization to shutdown, processing their input, updating their state, and rendering them. It also manages the scene's active status, controlling whether it is currently active and should be updated or rendered.
